Tricky ones

The issue with trying to use purely mnemonics is that your "image" might line up with some other characters. Make sure to learn the similar looking ones to avoid this happening as much as possible:

Similar looking Hiragana:

  • Face looking bois:

    Verさち ((ver) sa/chi)
    Kinda close: き (ki) and も (mo) and even ま (ma) to an extent
    Also ら (ra) looks like their cousin
  • Fishing ones:
    れ re / わ wa / ね ne
    (ready to fish / waaaa / net a fish)
  • Sideways U looking ones:
    う(u) vs つ (tsu)
  • "Knee" looking ones:
    The mnemonic for に (ni) is supposed to be "" に い ほ は け り

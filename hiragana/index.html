How to know if a mnemonic works for you Test yourself when you haven't studied in a while and see what comes back to you:
  • If nothing, the mnemonic probably wasn't strong enough visually.
  • If the wrong picture, it probably wasn't unique enough visually
  • If the wrong sound, it might be too confusing of a mnemonic. Mnemonics where you have to remember both an adjective and a noun are big offenders in this department.
    • One slightly confusing one I use is "21" for み - the sound is "mi", and the mnemonic is the phrase "I turned 21, that's me", but it's not "me" as in め. It's just because we pronounce "me" as "mi" in english unfortunately, so typing out み I'll mess up sometimes. Reading though luckily is unaffected
